Cambridge Primary Mathematics · Grade 3
  • Find horizontal and vertical lines of symmetry on 2D shapes

What is Symmetry?

A symmetrical shape can be split in two along a line of symmetry.

The line gives two equal halves that are mirror images of each other.

The line of symmetry is also called the mirror line.

Reflection

When you reflect a shape, you flip it across the mirror line.

Each point on the shape moves to the same distance on the other side of the mirror line.
